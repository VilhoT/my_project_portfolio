Electricity Price Scraping

Function

I've got a Python script running once every day at 15:01 (the next day's electricity prices are typically published by that time at the latest; Nordpool Electricity Price Data). It then parses the data, and once again publishes it to my MQTT server.

Now that that's been done, I could automate any and all devices individually or through Home Assistant to run based on the price of electricity, say, only charge the electric car when the price is lower than X or charge it when the price is at its lowest compared to the known later prices, or both of these. Or run some appliances at certain times.

Though, I have no electric car (nor a car) or use much electricity anyway, so much there's barely any real point for me to do any of this other than out of curiosity. That's why I'm simply using an ESP32 to light up some LEDs indicating the prices, mostly for fun. And to make sure the system works â€“ which it does (phew).
